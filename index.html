Essay on Cheating

Correlation does not always show causation. Because of the numerous different variables that actually result in a grade, because grades are correlated does not indicate that they are directly related to each other in terms of direct assistance to each other. This can relate to how much time they each spent on the homework, or how well they listen in class as well. Two students may be doing similarly but both poorly, so logically why would they cheat off each other when they both do not understand the material and coincidentally have a high correlation of grades. Students can also be highly negatively correlated, which migh suggest that some students would do the opposite of helping each other, i.e. sabotage.

A better way of trying to determine if students are cheating would be to find the similarities of their grades. Maybe check to see how many homework grades are exactly the same. The more exact same grades out of 50 points, the more likely two students are assisting each other.
